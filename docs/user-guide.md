## Getting Started

DesignFlow Kit is designed to streamline your UX/Product design workflow. The application organizes your design process into two main phases:

- **Pre-Design Phase**: Research, ideation, wireframing, and prototyping
- **Post-Design Phase**: Reviews, handover, testing, and documentation

### Accessing the Application

1. Visit the DesignFlow Kit website
2. Click **"Get Started"** to access the dashboard
3. No account required - your progress is saved locally in your browser

## Tracking Progress

DesignFlow Kit helps you track which design deliverables you've completed.

### How Progress Works

1. Each tool represents a deliverable in your design process
2. Click a tool to toggle its completion status
3. Progress is calculated per phase and overall
4. Visual indicators show completed vs. pending items

### Progress Indicators

- **Progress Bar**: Shows percentage complete for each phase
- **Completion Count**: Displays "X of Y completed"
- **Visual Checkmarks**: Completed items show a checkmark

### Resetting Progress

To start fresh:
1. Look for the **"Reset Progress"** button on the dashboard
2. Confirm the reset action
3. All progress will be cleared

## Data Persistence

Your progress and preferences are saved automatically.

### How Storage Works

- **localStorage**: Progress is saved to your browser's local storage
- **No Account Required**: Your data stays on your device
- **Automatic Saving**: Changes are saved immediately

### What Gets Saved

| Data | Storage |
|------|---------|
| Tool completion status | localStorage |
| Theme preference (light/dark) | localStorage |
| Progress percentages | Calculated from completion status |

### Clearing Your Data

To clear all saved data:

**Option 1: Use Reset Button**
- Click "Reset Progress" on the dashboard

**Option 2: Clear Browser Data**
1. Open browser settings
2. Clear site data for DesignFlow Kit
3. Refresh the page

### Limitations

- Data is browser-specific (won't sync across devices)
- Clearing browser data will remove progress
- Private/Incognito mode won't persist data

## Tips for Success

### For Beginners
1. Start with the Pre-Design tools in order
2. Watch video tutorials before diving in
3. Use the integrations to connect your favorite tools

### For Teams
1. Share tool details pages for onboarding
2. Use consistent processes across the team
3. Contribute to the open-source project

### Best Practices
1. Complete research before moving to wireframes
2. Test early with Lo-Fi designs
3. Don't skip the handover process
4. Document everything in Process Docs
